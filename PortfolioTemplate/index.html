    Expertise

    Field Archaeology

    I have participated in or managed numerous archaeological surveys, testing projects, and subsurface data recovery operations. Most of these projects have required in-depth artifact analyses and detailed field mapping using mobile global positioning systems. I have strong communication skills, and during the course of these projects, I have coordinated with numerous agencies including client representatives, Native American Groups, and State Historic Preservation Officers. I also have experience with SB-18/AB-52 Native American consultations specific to the state of California.

    Research and Report Writing

    I am experienced in conducting detailed research for the development of reports and cultural resource evaluations for the National Register of Historic Places under the National Historic Preservation Act, and the California Register of Historical Resources under the California Environmental Quality Act.

    Geographic Analysis

    I am proficient with geographic information systems (GIS) and analyses, as well as interactive mapping and data visualization. Some of the GIS analyses I have experience with include point pattern analysis for the examination of site distributions, least-cost / optimal path analysis for determining potential historic routes, and predictive modeling. Proficient with HTML, JavaScript, and CSS coding applications.
